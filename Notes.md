## Design Notes & Decisions

**Resolved — moved to gamerequirements.md:**
- Upgrade choice (2 orbs, pick 1) → added as "Upgrade Choice" under Passive Upgrades.
- Risk/reward scoring (Close Call, Last Second) → added under Scoring Improvements.
- Mario Kart item roulette system → fully specced as Item Roulette System with 2-slot queue, dual acquisition, weighted randomness.
- Gravity well surfacing → resolved as a roulette item activated via right-click.
- Menu button pausing → resolved: HUD buttons do NOT pause the game.

**Decided against:**
- Space bar to toggle between shield types — replaced by the roulette item system which gives more variety without overloading shield placement controls.

**Future ideas (parked):**
